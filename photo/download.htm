Need a new laptop? Shop our top picks from HP's massive Labor Day sale

Melissa Lee
Reviewed.com

— Recommendations are independently chosen by Reviewed’s editors. Purchases you make through our links may earn us a commission.

Labor Day is just a week away (it's Monday, September 7, FYI!) and with the holiday comes plenty of backyard barbecuing, poolside lounging, and, of course, a ton of incredible sales. From appliances to mattresses, deal hunters can majorly save on those big-ticket items that they’ve been eyeing all year long—including laptops, printers and more. Ladies and gentlemen, we give you HP’s Labor Day sale.

Through Saturday, September 12, the tech giant is offering substantial deals on its large stock of top-quality electronics: There's even additional discounts available on select items. For instance, when you buy certain PC models, you'll save 15% on accessories, keyboards and more, while McAfee anti-virus security is free with any laptop, desktop or PC. You can also snag free shipping and easy returns with each order.

Below, check out seven on the most exciting deals available at HP for its huge Labor Day savings bonanza. 

1. $20 off: A 12-month subscription of Microsoft 365

Microsoft 365 is an incredibly useful program for just about anyone who frequently uses a laptop, computer or desktop. This 12-month subscription encompasses Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook and more, all for $49.99, which is $20 less than its usual price of $69.99. The digital download is built for Androids, Macs, Windows and iOS systems, plus, it has a collective 4.1-star rating from HP customers. We even included it in our roundup of HP products every remote student needs.

Get the Microsoft 365 Personal 12-month Subscription for $49.99 (Save $20)

2. Save $200: This awesome laptop with a gorgeous design

The HP Spectre x360 model is an extremely beloved laptop here at Reviewed. We’ve  tested out many editions over the years, but the 13t—a.k.a. the most recent variant that HP has released—is, quite simply, jaw-dropping. From its seriously beautiful display to the user-friendly design, we couldn’t stop raving about this computer in our 2020 review. The 13-inch laptop includes impressive features, such as its 10th generation Intel i7 core processor, 512 gigabytes (GB) of storage, 8GB of memory and an integrated graphics card. In testing, the battery lasted just under eight hours. The port selection, meanwhile, consists of two USB-Cs and one larger USB-A. This incredible laptop normally retails from $1,229.99, but when you shop the site’s Labor Day sale, you can snag it for $1,029.99, giving you a total of $200 in savings. 

Get the HP Spectre x360 Laptop 13t for $1,029.99 (Save $200)

3. $100 in savings: One of our favorite laptops for students

Students on the hunt for back-to-school tech can end their search here: The HP Omen 15, which we hailed as one of the best laptops for students, is currently on sale for $1,549.99, down $100 from its retail price of $1,649.99. In testing, we liked a lot of the Omen 15’s features, namely, the beautiful display, which boasts a 15.6-inch diagonal full HD screen. It also comes with an edgier, more fun design, thanks to the black shell and red accents. In terms of specs, this model includes Windows 10 Home 64, a 9th generation Intel core i7 processor, 16 GB of memory, 1 terabyte (TB) of hard disk drive (HDD) storage and 512 GB of solid-state drive (SSD) storage. 

Get the HP Omen 15 for $1,549.99 (Save $100) 

4. Less than $30: This super durable mouse pad

HP’s massive holiday sale also extends to accessories, like the Omen by HP mouse pad 200, once $31.99, and now $22.20 with the $9.79 discount. (Note that you can get it for $0.21 cheaper at Walmart.) We featured the 100 version (currently out of stock) in our list of the best HP products for at-home studying, as our tester found it to be super comfortable. There are a few differences between the two—for instance, the 200 is less cushy and is made from a harder plastic material, as opposed to the 100's plushy fabric. The 200 also utilizes ultra-low surface friction, allowing you to move the mouse with minimal effort. The consistent surface lets the mouse sensors easily translate your movement, meaning you'll ultimately use less force and effort to click, surf and scroll. Additionally, when you buy select discounted accessories at HP, you can nab another one for 15% off. 

Get the Omen by HP Mouse Pad 200 for $22.20 (Save $9.79)

5. A $240 markdown: This laptop with nearly 500 rave reviews

Still in the market for the perfect laptop? Check out the HP 15t laptop, which has a 4.1-star rating from more than 490 buyers. Although we haven’t yet tested this model, the specs on this computer are pretty impressive: It comes with Windows 10 Home 64, a 10th generation Intel core i5 processor, Intel UHD graphics, 12GB of memory, 256GB of solid-state drive storage and a 15.6-inch HD display. Available in three colors (silver, gold and white), customers rave about its top-notch performance, compact size (it weighs just 3.85 pounds!) and lengthy battery life, which can recharge halfway in 45 minutes. According to Jon Chan, our lab manager, this laptop is perfect for those that like to browse the Internet and watch movies. He also noted that the "15-inch display and full keyboard [are] a perfect combo for writing term papers—or not." The HP 15t normally retails from $789.99, but you can shop the site’s massive holiday sale to snag it for $549.99, giving you $240 in savings.

Get the HP 15t Laptop for $549.99 (Save $240)

6. Save $200: This powerful gaming laptop

Also on sale is the HP Pavilion 15 gaming laptop. This machine was once $1,099.99 but is now $899.99 with the $200 discount. Featured in our roundup of the best gaming laptops under $1,000, the keyboard, graphics card and price point were all impressive facets that led to its placement on the list, despite the fact that we weren't thrilled with the aesthetic design. In testing, this laptop even outperformed the HP Omen 17 (also on sale for $1,499.99), thanks to its streaming abilities and graphics. The HP Pavilion 15 comes with a 10th generation Intel core i5 processor, along with 16GB of memory, 1TB of hard disk drive (HDD) storage and a 15.6-inch full HD display. 

Get the HP Pavilion 15 Gaming Laptop for $899.99 (Save $200)

7. Less than $90: This portable photo printer 

Last but certainly not least is the HP Sprocket 2nd edition photo printer, which is typically priced at $129.99 and is now $89.99, falling by 31% We included the HP Sprocket 100 (currently out of stock) in our list of the best portable photo printers because of its quick and easy loading and printing process. The 2nd generation version is similar but includes even more impressive features, like its lighter weight (it’s only .38 pounds versus the 100's .40 pounds, making it super portable) and Bluetooth connectivity. The HP Sprocket 2nd edition also has a collective 4.1-star rating from more than 140 HP shoppers, who loved its compact size and short printing time. 

Get the HP Sprocket 2nd Edition Photo Printer for $89.99 (Save $40)
